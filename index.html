"Welcome everyone"

"Education is the finest gift to be given to someone. A educated person not only adorns his/her life but also leaves behind a enlightened path for the generations to come."


Avantikulam is a project aimed at providing free online education for NTSE/Olympiad, Jee/Neet aspirants.There are many people in our surroundings who do not have accesss to education or do not have enough money to complete their education. We as a human can help these people by providing education in the form of teaching the children or providing them the necessary resources required to excel in that field.

"Join this movement"

"Education is for improving the lives of others and for leaving your community and world better than you found it."


Avantikulam, an initiative of INSPIRE MANIT to provide free education to under previlaged students. Over the journey of 4 years we have covered nearly 3500+ students from various KVs,JNVs and many other rural areas across MP. We now aim to expand our network to reach more and more students. Join us in this movement where all of us will provide education to those who are underprivileged.
